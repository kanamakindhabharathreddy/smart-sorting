What is SmartSort?

SmartSort is a cutting-edge project that leverages Artificial Intelligence to classify the freshness of produce. By simply uploading an image, our model can determine if a fruit or vegetable is fresh or rotten, helping to reduce food waste at both consumer and industrial levels.

Easy to Use

Just snap a photo. Our intuitive interface makes freshness detection a breeze for everyone.

AI-Powered

Utilizes a deep learning model trained on thousands of images for high accuracy.

Global Impact

Aims to significantly reduce the 1.3 billion tonnes of food wasted globally each year.
